To Track Daily Expenses

Simplify allows for setting a budget and managing day-to-day spending categories. You can quickly and easily see if you are over or under budget. The app also has a social feature for shared expenses. You can start groups amongst your peers to manage budget, track and sync together to reach shared goals. You’ll enjoy a consolidated view of joint accounts.

Sync Routines

Share your calendars with different groups and connect with peers. Simplify makes it effortless to coordinate and collaborate with others in a single calendar. Stay in sync and plan activities together as per everyone's routines.

To-Do List

All of us have multiple tasks to be completed on a day-to-day basis. Noting them down and keeping a track of them gives you accountability and helps you stay in track. Simplify makes it easy to manage your daily task list, especially in an Agile Way.
